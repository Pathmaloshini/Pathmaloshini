Manchester United Triumphs Over Chelsea with Dominant 3-1 Victory

"Rashford Scores Twice as United Tightens Grip on Premier League Title Race"
2024.08.18

On August 18, 2024, Manchester United secured a crucial 3-1 victory over Chelsea in the Premier League. Marcus Rashford led the charge with two goals, while Bruno Fernandes added a third. Despite a late push from Chelsea, United's defense held strong, boosting their title race ambitions and fueling fans' optimism for the season.
